Pricing questions

Is Pro really a one-time purchase?+
Yes. $14.99 once, yours forever — no subscription, no renewals, no "annual plan." You buy it through the App Store as a non-consumable in-app purchase and it's tied to your Apple ID.
Does Family Sharing work?+
It does. One purchase covers everyone in your Apple Family Sharing group, at no extra cost.
What's the watermark on the free version?+
In the free version, a small, tasteful Wideframe mark is added to every image Wideframe produces — both files you export and wallpapers you set on your desktop. It's a discreet corner mark, never burned across the picture. Wideframe Pro removes it entirely, everywhere.
Can I try Pro features before buying?+
The whole editor is free, so you can frame, preview and set wallpapers all you like before deciding. Pro unlocks the library, multi-monitor spanning and scheduling — and you can buy it the moment you hit one of those.
Do you offer refunds?+
Refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store. If something isn't right, request a refund via reportaproblem.apple.com — or email support@wideframe.studio and we'll help.
